Shredded Chicken

Description

This dish packs protein and flavor, all into one powerful punch.

Be sure to put on your dancing shoes because this dish is guaranteed to knock your socks off.

Ingredients

  • Pot
  • Chicken
  • Shredder
  • Exotic Spices & Salsa
  • Clove of Garlic

Steps

  1. Take your pot and place it on the table.
  2. Empty the contents of the chicken.
  3. Add your rare and exotic spices.
  4. Put on the stove and let simmer.
  5. Leave it for 55 minutes, and then return to your morsel.
