Lasagna

Description

This is a delectable dish, consisting of a symphony of flavors. The meat, the cheese, the protein, fats and carbs ... they all coalesce to make something that's truly extraordinary.

Keep reading below, to see how to recreate this tasty morsel for yourself.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef: bovine or fowl. Whichever you prefer.
  • 4 cheeses: the more extravagant the better.
  • Salt, pepper & other rare Arabian spices.
  • Pasta to layer the lasagna.
  • Tomato sauce to create the symphony of flavors.

Steps

  1. Get your baking dish ready.
  2. Spray the dish or rub generously with olive oil.
  3. Layer the bottom with pasta, add ground beef, then tomato sauce and then cheese.
  4. Repeat the above step 4 more times, so that we have a staircase of flavors cascading into one another.
  5. Generously add cheese to the top.
  6. Turn the oven to 450 degrees, and bake for 1-hour.
