Tuna Salad

Description

This delectable treat, is sure to get the party started.

Fresh from the Atlantic, this fish is sure to please: just make sure to have some gum for after. Or fresh ginger, like they do in Japan with sushi.

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Salad
  • Bread
  • Mayonaise
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Get a bowl.
  2. Pour in tuna.
  3. Add mayonaise.
  4. Add a dab of mustard.
  5. Mix and enjoy.
